The plan — 4 phases

1

Build the database

Week 1

Set up Supabase. Four tables: listings, vendors, bookings, service_requests. Nothing else. Then manually fill it: find 5 real furnished rentals in Yerevan, 3 real cleaners, 2 food services. Call them. Confirm they can work with you.

Supabase · No frontend yet · Pure data
2

Build the booking UI

Weeks 2–3

Next.js. A listings page. A booking form. An admin panel for you to see requests and change statuses. That's it. Users can browse listings and submit a booking. You receive a notification. You handle it manually — WhatsApp the landlord, confirm the cleaner, arrange the food. You are the backend for now.

Next.js · Supabase · No payments yet
3

Add payment + first users

Month 2

Connect Stripe. One subscription plan. When payment goes through, booking activates. Find 2–3 users from your AVC network — digital nomads, people you already know. Give them access. Operate manually. Learn every single thing that breaks or feels wrong.

Stripe · AVC network · Wizard-of-Oz ops
4

Automate → Decentralize

Month 3+

Replace yourself with code. Webhooks to vendor APIs. Auto-dispatch on booking confirmation. Auto-split payments to landlord + cleaner + food vendor. Now add the DAO layer — smart contracts for fund management, community curation of vendor quality, multi-sig treasury.

Webhooks · Auto-dispatch · Smart contracts · DAO governance
